Moment 01 · Arrival
The scan that replaces the front desk question.
Confirmed tee time, course conditions, who they're playing with — before anyone has to look anything up. Staff sees the same confirmation. One scan does both jobs.
Utility first · Zero friction
Moment 02 · Discovery
The club teaches itself to new members.
Pickleball 101. American Mahjong. Reciprocal club benefits. Every club has knowledge that lives only in staff heads — ClubOS Pass puts it on any screen, at any hour, from any QR in the building.
Inform before engage
Moment 03 · Events
RSVP from wherever they're standing.
Member sees the event on the dining room TV, scans, and reserves their spot — without walking to the desk or remembering to call Monday. The scan already knows which screen they came from.
Location-aware · Instant
Moment 04 · Concierge
One character. Every question.
Ruby is the club's AI guide — dining specials, court availability, upcoming events, tee times. The more a member uses it, the more Ruby learns their preferences. Always opt-in, never required.
Always-on intelligence
Moment 05 · Your Pass
Member Pass. Guest Pass. Event Pass. Same infrastructure.
A Member Pass carries the full club relationship. A Guest Pass gives a member's guest a measured experience without needing membership. An Event Pass is scoped to one occasion — check-in, course details, the evening's flow. White-labeled to the club. Owned by the club.
Member · Guest · Event
Moment 06 · Guest & Event Experience
A prospective member's first real experience of the club.
A guest at Wine & Dine gets an Event Pass scoped to the evening. Their QR handles check-in. The course detail is built in. And a quiet note at the bottom: every scan tonight is already building their profile. When they're ready to explore membership, the club already knows them. That's a different kind of pipeline.
Acquisition · Not just retention
Worth saying clearly
The competitive layer is optional. The intelligence layer is always on. The same infrastructure that runs a live trivia leaderboard runs a quiet guest experience or a private event pass. Every pass is the club — just scoped to who's holding it.
