The Entry Point
The moment before the club opens.
A QR code on any screen in the club. Member points their camera. No app store, no login screen — ClubOS Pass opens instantly, already knowing where they are and what's happening now.
No download · No friction
Moment 01 · Arrival
The scan that replaces the front desk question.
Confirmed tee time, course conditions, who they're playing with — before anyone has to look anything up. Staff sees the same confirmation. One scan does both jobs.
Utility first · Zero friction
Moment 02 · Discovery
The club teaches itself to new members.
Pickleball 101. American Mahjong. Reciprocal club benefits. Every club has knowledge that lives only in staff heads — this puts it on the screen, at any hour, from any QR in the building.
Inform before engage
Moment 03 · Events
RSVP from wherever they're standing.
Member sees an event notice on the dining room TV, scans, and reserves their spot — without walking to the desk or remembering to call Monday. The scan knows which screen they came from.
Location-aware · Instant
Moment 04 · Concierge
One character. Every question.
Ruby is the club's AI guide — dining specials, court availability, upcoming events, tee times. Ask anything. The more a member uses it, the more Ruby learns their preferences. Always opt-in, never required.
Always-on intelligence
Moment 05 · Profile
Anonymous by default. Remembered when they choose.
Every scan quietly builds a picture — no account needed. When a member is ready, one tap links their activity to their club membership. Their data stays with the club. Always. No exceptions.
Privacy by design
The Progression
Confirm → Inform → Participate. The first scan earns trust by doing something useful. The second delivers value. By the third, the member is engaged — not because they were asked to be, but because the experience gave them a reason. That loop sustains itself.
